About Svasys Inc.

Svasys Inc. is a technology company focused on cryptographic identity and authentication. We build security solutions for mobile and web platforms.

What We Do

We develop authentication technologies for organizations that require strong identity verification. Our solutions work across iOS, Android, and web browsers.

Our Approach

We build on established cryptographic standards to deliver reliable security infrastructure. Our focus is on correctness, auditability, and cross-platform consistency.

Company

Svasys Inc. is a privately held, Delaware-incorporated company. The name "SVA" derives from the Sanskrit word sva (स्व), meaning "self" — reflecting our focus on identity.
